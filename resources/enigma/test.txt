Hello dear reader,

This is a test of my new CLI tool, enigma, which encrypts (-e) and decrypts (-d) text or
files. The project is inspired by the Enigma machine used in Nazi Germany from the 1920s
to the 1940s.

Cryptography has always fascinated me, and understanding Enigma is almost unavoidable in
that field. As I researched and implemented the mechanics, the project became more than
technical -  it became personal.

Alan Turing's story affected me deeply. Despite his brilliance and his role in breaking
Enigma, he was persecuted under discriminatory British laws criminalizing homosexuality.
He was chemically castrated and later died in circumstances widely believed to be suicide.
It is tragic that even today, despite our technological progress, we still struggle with
basic humanity.

My enigma engine is not a replica but an homage - a reminder of the price that brilliance
often carries. I also included a reference to the Club 27, honoring artists whose
extraordinary talent ended too soon.

Let's ensure that people like Alan Turing, Jimi Hendrix, Amy Winehouse, and Kurt Cobain
are remembered for their contributions, not judged for their identities.

Cheers,
Patrik Eigenmann
